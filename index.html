Simple Omlette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classes omlette combines beaten eggs coocked to perfection, optionaly filled width your choise of cheese, vegatables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximitaly 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter oil
  • Optional filling: cheese, diced vegetables, coocked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier textur.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non steack frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pant to the ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add filling (optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly running at the middle, sprinckle you chosen filling over one half of the omellette.
  5. Fold and serve: As the omellete continius to cook, carefuly lift one adge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
